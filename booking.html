Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by contacting us via email or phone. Please provide your contact information, a brief description of the tattoo design, and your preferred appointment date and time.

What should I do to prepare for my tattoo session?

Make sure to get a good night's sleep, stay hydrated, and eat a meal before your session. Avoid alcohol and blood-thinning medications for at least 24 hours prior to your appointment.

How much does a tattoo cost?

The cost of a tattoo depends on the size, complexity, and location of the design. Please contact us for a consultation and a personalized quote.

Do you offer touch-ups?

Yes, I offer free touch-ups within the first 3 months after your tattoo session. After that, touch-ups are available for a fee.

How should I care for my new tattoo?

Keep the tattoo clean and moisturized, avoid direct sunlight, and refrain from swimming or soaking the tattoo for at least two weeks. Detailed aftercare instructions will be provided during your appointment.

Do tattoos hurt?

The pain level varies depending on the tattoo's location and your pain tolerance. I strive to make the experience as comfortable as possible.

Can I bring my own design?

Yes, you can bring your own design or collaborate to create a custom piece.

What is your cancellation policy?

I require at least 48 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Failure to do so may result in a cancellation fee.
