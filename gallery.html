The Siren's Muse Studio

Hello and welcome to Siren's Muse Tattoo, where your unique vision is transformed into stunning body art. I'm Aurelia Hawthorne, the owner and sole artist here. My studio is a sanctuary of creativity and precision, tailored to provide a genuinely personal tattoo experience that values your individuality.

Siren's Muse Tattoo studio opened in 2019 after 12 years in the industry left me with a deep desire to establish a space that truly reflects my own tattoo journey and aesthetic vision. My goal was to create not just a tattoo studio, but a welcoming, comfortable environment where everyone who walks through the door feels at ease and inspired, ready to embark on their unique tattoo journey.

Booking is simple via our website, where you can arrange an appointment or drop by for a free consultation. Located conveniently, my studio offers a welcoming, comfortable environment to make your tattoo journey as enjoyable and relaxing as possible. I cherish your feedback and continuously strive to improve the quality of my service. Please feel free to share your thoughts with me. Visit Siren's Muse Tattoo, where each piece of art reflects your unique self.
