Welcome to Siren’s Muse

I’m Aurelia Hawthorne, the artist behind Siren’s Muse Tattoo. I combine the mystical allure of sirens with modern design to create personalized, technically precise tattoos.

As a single-artist studio, I focus on a comfortable, safe, and collaborative experience—from consultation through aftercare—so your idea becomes art that lasts.
